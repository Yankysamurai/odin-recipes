Mapo Tofu

Description

Mapo Tofu is a spicy Chinese dish that stir fries tofu, ground pork, garlic and ginger and is served over hot jasmine rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablesoons olive oil
  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1 (14 ounce) package of firm tofu, cubed
  • 1 tablespoon spicy black bean sauce
  • soy sauce to taste
  • 2 tablespoons dry sherry
  • 1 bunch green onions, chopped

Steps

  1. Cook garlic in olive oil over medium heat until lightly browned
  2. Add ground pork and brown, stirring frequently
  3. Stir in tofu, reduce heat to medium, cook for three minutes
  4. Season with black bean sauce, soy sauce, sherry and green onions
  5. Serve over rice
