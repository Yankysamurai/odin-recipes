Szechuan Beef

Description

Szechuan style cooking is famous for its bold flavours including liberal amounts of garlic, chilli peppers and Szechuan pepper if available.

This dish combines marinated beef with broccoli in a delicious Szechuan sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sirloin steak, cut into bite size strips
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red chilli pepper (lots more is OK)
  • 1 clove garlic, minced (or more)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cups fresh broccoli florets
  • 1 (8 ounce) can water chestnuts, drained
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 2 small onions
  • Szechuan pepper(optional)
  • 1/2 cup peanuts (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons green onions, chopped

Steps

  1. Mix beef with soy sauce, cornstarch, crushed red peppers, Szechuan pepper and garlic in bowl. Cover and refrigerate 20 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in wok or large pan over high heat.
  3. Stir fry beef until no longer pink, 5 minutes
  4. Stir in broccoli, onions and water chestnuts, cook 2 minutes
  5. Pour in broth and bring to a boil
  6. Stir in peanuts, cook 1 minute and serve with rice
  7. Garnish with green onions
