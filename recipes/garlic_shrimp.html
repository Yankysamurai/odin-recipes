Spicy Chinese Garlic Shrimp

Description

If you love garlic, shrimp and a spicy asian stir fry this is the perfect dish for you. My whole family loves it!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 10 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 (8 ounce) can sliced water chestnuts, drained
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 1 cup small white button mushrooms
  • 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (or lots more per taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground blak pepper
  • 1 pound peeled and deveined jumbo shrimp
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons dry sherry
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon water

Steps

  1. Combine the chicken broth, rice vinegar, fish sauce and dry sherry in a small bowl. Set aside for later.
  2. Heat oil in wok pr large pan until very hot.
  3. Cook and stir garlic and ginger in the hot oil until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  4. Add the water chestnuts, snow peas, mushrooms, red pepper flakes, salt, pepper and shrimp to the pan. Cook, stirring, until shrimp turns pink, 2 to 3 minutes.
  5. Take chicken broth mixture from step 1 and add to the pan, cook and stir briefly to combine.
  6. Combine the cornstarch and water and stir into the pan. Stir until the sauce has thickened, about 2 minutes.
  7. Serve with white rice.
